About Us

Welcome to the Garuda Plot Owners Association official website!

We are delighted to have you here. Our association is dedicated to enhancing the living experience in Garuda Plot Owners Association, Tirupati by fostering a sense of community, addressing common concerns, and working collaboratively towards shared goals.

Association formed with registration No. 444/2019. We are located at Plot No. 65, Agri Gold Layout, Garudadri Nagar, Chennayagunta Village, Tirupati, Andhra Pradesh.

We have about 122 plots as per layout plan and TUDA Approved Layout vide: L.P.No.42/G1/07, dated: 18-12-2007, situated in Survey No. 213(P) of Chennaiahgunta village of Settipalle Gram Panchayat,Tirupati Rural Mandal and SRO Renigunta.

Issues

  • Our Properties are attached by CID and blocked for registrations since 2015.
  • An outsider has come into our venture set up a pig farm and installed a feeding fence inside park area.
  • Some plots in the layout are currently submerged in water. We kindly request the affected plot owners to come forward and coordinate with neighboring plot owners to facilitate drainage and clear the water.Your cooperation will help ensure a safer and healthier environment for everyone.
  • As you may know our layout was abandoned for many years and all the infrastructure installed earlier are destroyed or stolen, it is high time for everyone to come together for the development of our layout.
